Tentang Gerakan Ini About This Movement

Sumberejo 2027 merupakan gerakan perubahan menuju desa yang lebih maju melalui pelayanan publik yang profesional, pembangunan berkelanjutan, pemberdayaan masyarakat, serta transparansi pemerintahan.

Sumberejo 2027 is a movement dedicated to creating a modern, transparent, and prosperous village through sustainable development, professional public services, and community empowerment.

InisiatifInitiatives

Program UnggulanFeatured Programs

Solusi inovatif untuk meningkatkan kualitas hidup masyarakat dan memajukan infrastruktur desa.

Innovative solutions to improve the quality of life and advance village infrastructure.

🏡 Smart Village

Digitalisasi administrasi desa untuk pelayanan yang lebih cepat, transparan, dan dapat diakses darimana saja.

Digitization of village administration for faster, transparent, and accessible services from anywhere.

📚 Pendidikan Berkualitas

Bantuan beasiswa, peningkatan fasilitas sekolah dasar, dan program literasi digital untuk anak desa.

Scholarship assistance, improvement of elementary school facilities, and digital literacy programs.

🌱 Pertanian Modern

Penerapan teknologi pertanian modern, bantuan pupuk subsidi tepat sasaran, dan akses pasar luas.

Implementation of modern agricultural tech, targeted subsidized fertilizers, and broad market access.

💼 UMKM Digital

Pelatihan e-commerce, bantuan modal usaha, dan pembentukan marketplace khusus produk unggulan desa.

E-commerce training, business capital assistance, and creation of a marketplace for village products.

🚑 Pelayanan Kesehatan

Peningkatan fasilitas polindes, program gizi balita (stunting nol), dan layanan ambulans desa 24 jam.

Improvement of village health facilities, toddler nutrition programs, and 24-hour ambulance service.

🌳 Green Village

Pengelolaan sampah terpadu (bank sampah), penghijauan, dan pemanfaatan energi ramah lingkungan.

Integrated waste management, reforestation, and utilization of environmentally friendly energy.
